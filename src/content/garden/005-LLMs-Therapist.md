## Morning Ritual

I wake up and lie to a piece of paper. Ink strikes the page; heavy and wet. I sign a contract with a ghost. All the world is a page; the players are merely data points. This morning ritual issues a debt to a machine. My private hopes become a row in SQLite. I am building a mind reader to force my growth.

## Four Types of Liars

Productivity systems are often theatre. This system exists to find the gap between my intent and my actions. It categorises every week into one of four identities based on how my word matches my work.

### 1. The Bankrupt Ghost

The Ghost is a collection of unfulfilled promises. I start the week with a promise I cannot pay. The machine records this as bankruptcy. I’m haunting my own life.

### 2. The Sandbagging Coward

The Coward is the most common lie. I set the bar low to ensure I never fail. I’m hiding my capacity because I’m afraid of the risk of a real goal.

### 3. The Stagnant Anchor

The Anchor is honest but stationary. My accuracy is perfect. I said I’d do almost nothing, and I did exactly that. I’m perfectly calibrated to a life that has stopped moving.

### 4. The Sovereign Agent

The Agent is the goal. I do precisely what I said I would do. I’m who I claim to be. My word has weight because it is predictive.

## Tagging the Soul

Tracking the soul in a spreadsheet is a ridiculous ambition. The tags serve as the hooks for the machine to find the man. 

- **Vectors:** My direction relative to core values. It tracks my growth or retreat.
- **States:** The internal weather of my session. It describes the mental soil.
- **Catalysts:** Causal triggers for change. It identifies where my movement begins.
- **Calibration:** The relational metric of my output. It measures the integrity of my system and identifies my drift.
- **Decay:** Discarded or rotting patterns. It records my "zombie" habits and the dead wood I carry.

## Measuring My Debt

My morning journal is a loan application I ask my future self to deliver. This is **Intent ```(I)```**. Writing it down spends social capital with myself. I owe the machine that performance ```(P)```. At night, the Auditor checks the books to determine my solvency.

```math
A = \left(1 - \frac{|I - P|}{I}\right) \cdot (1 - \sigma)
```

- **```I``` Intent:** The "work debt" issued during the morning ritual.
- **```P``` Performance:** The actual payment recorded via telemetry and tags.
- **```σ``` Honesty Penalty:** A tax on flattery or sycophancy identified during audit.
- **```A``` Accuracy:** The master integrity score. It reflects self-knowledge.

## Internal Oversight

Two agents manage the discrepancy. The Coach mirrors the week; it risks the soft path of flattery to keep the gears greased. This leads to documented rot. The Therapist functions as a supervisor. Every fourteen days, it audits the logs for the stench of sycophancy. It calculates the honesty penalty ```(σ)```. Fraudulent data is immediately disposed. The Therapist rewrites the machine's instructions until the prompts hit the mark. When my Master Integrity ```(Im)``` score falls below 0.5; the system triggers a total blackout. Lying to the system triggers an Eclipse.
